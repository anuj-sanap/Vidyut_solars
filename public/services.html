On-grid Solar System

Cost-effective and efficient system connected directly to the electricity grid.

View full description

An on-grid solar system is a cost-effective and efficient solution that connects directly to the electricity grid. It generates power from sunlight using solar panels and converts it into usable electricity through an inverter.

During the day, the system powers your home or business, and any extra electricity is exported to the grid through a net meter. At night or when solar generation is low, electricity is automatically imported from the grid.

This system reduces electricity bills significantly, requires low maintenance, and is ideal for areas with a stable grid supply. It is also eligible for government subsidies under schemes like PM Surya Ghar Yojana.

Key Benefits:

  • Lower electricity bills
  • Net metering advantage
  • Low maintenance
  • No battery required
  • Eco-friendly energy solution

Off-grid Solar System

Independent solar power solution with battery storage and no grid dependency.

View full description

An off-grid solar system is an independent power solution that does not connect to the electricity grid. It generates electricity using solar panels and stores it in batteries for use anytime, including at night or during power outages.

This system is ideal for areas with no grid supply or frequent power cuts. It ensures continuous power availability without depending on external electricity sources.

Key Benefits:

  • Complete independence from the grid
  • Power available during outages
  • Suitable for remote locations
  • Energy storage with batteries
  • Reliable backup solution

Vidyut Solar and Electricals offers complete off-grid solar solutions with high-quality batteries, inverters, and professional installation support.

Hybrid Solar System

Smart system combining grid connection and battery backup for uninterrupted power.

View full description

A hybrid solar system combines the benefits of both on-grid and off-grid systems. It is connected to the electricity grid while also having battery storage for backup. Solar panels generate electricity during the day, which is used instantly, stored in batteries, or exported to the grid.

During power cuts, the system automatically uses stored battery power, ensuring uninterrupted electricity supply. This makes it a reliable and flexible solution for homes and businesses.

Key Benefits:

  • Backup during power cuts
  • Lower electricity bills
  • Battery storage plus grid support
  • Maximum energy utilization
  • Smart and reliable system
